Engagement with Life

Master Plan Evaluator

The Problem

For several weekends recently I've felt a profound depression and unsatisfaction with life. As I've contemplated it, I've recognized that it arises from a apathetic lack of engagement with life. I've began to go through the motions without much that invigorates and excites me. I need to rehab my sense of purpose and reignite my drive for making my type of mark on the world.

The Path to the Solution

I'm in a season of contemplation and prayer, and I know the right approach will emerge given careful consideration, discussion with trusted advisors, and time. I know the solution will be found in new applications of very elemental and well known aspects of my personality, talents, and needs.

While there is always more to learn and interpret, I'm excited about this contemplative season because I've particularized more aspects of what have made fulfilling projects work for me. Below are some of the key realizations of reflection in this season:

  • Simple Location

    Community should have Simple Location â€“ a standing time and place available to them to engage easily without logistics when they need or want it.

  • Emergence

    Community interactions can happen spontaneously and in unexpected, unplanned ways, unencumbered by the structure and logistics required to organize formal overly agenda-ed and scheduled meetings and events.

  • Headfake

    Often if the goal is explicit, expectations can actually confuse and muddle, greatly interfering. If the real goal is inadvertantly accomplished in the pursuit of some other goal, it tends to work much better. Worthwhile change has to be snuck up upon.

  • Leverage

    I need to have something tangible to offer -- some resource or skill that I'm depended on to provide. I provide this ostensible solution with grace and aplomb, all the while REALLY offering something more substantial and meaningful under the surface.

  • Partnership

    It's only worth doing if it can be done with someone else. This doesn't mean someone has to join me, it works also if I can find something someone else is doing and join up. Finding the right partner(s) is key.

  • Depth / Meaning

    Lack of this is why I eventually gave up on Another Story (possibly prematurely, and I didn't realize why until much later). I need there to be some greater purpose, some important and meaningful human growth, improvement, or insight happening as an outgrowth of the effort.

  • Public

    Some aspect of the project should occur in public. It should have a target audience and face the intrigue and risk of engaging or failing to engage that target audience in public.
